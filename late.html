—Late Policy

There is no credit for any late homework.

I will present the solutions to some of the assignments right after they are due. Some others will build atop previous assignments. Hence all assignments must be turned in on time.

Why else? This is a matter of social responsibility. You are responsible to the rest of your development team, your clients, and the broader society depending on you to deliver correct software, and on-time. Why else? Late delivery costs money, and can have critical downstream impacts.
